Voice Isolation

Upload any audio or video and our AI separates the voice track from background noise, music, and ambiance with surgical precision.

🎬
Audio & Video Input

Supports MP3, M4A, WAV, AAC, FLAC, OGG and more for audio. MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV and more for video. Up to 5 minutes for audio, 2 minutes for video.

🎚
Three Playback Modes

Play the isolated voice, the original audio, or silence. Switch instantly — perfect for comparing before and after.

⏺
Built-in Recorder

Record audio directly in the app — up to 5 minutes — and process it immediately. No need to import from files.

📥
Download & Share

Save isolated voice files directly to your Music library or share them with any app on your device instantly.

🗂
Studio Archive

Every processed file is saved to your Studio tab. Access, replay, download, or delete your history at any time.

Process
How it works
01 ──────
📂
Import or Record

Tap + to upload an audio file, a video, or record directly using the built-in recorder.

02 ──────
⚙️
AI Processing

NoNoise sends your file to our secure Cloud server. Our AI engine processes it and isolates the voice track. This typically takes 1–3 minutes.

03 ──────
✅
Get Your Voice

Download the clean isolated voice track as an MP3. Share it, use it, delete it — it's yours.

04 ──────

Processed files on our server are deleted after download. Nothing persists on our end.
